Welcome To The Ghana Islamic Society for Education & Reformation

Dear Brothers and Sisters, Praise be to Allah the Creator of the whole world. Blessing and peace be upon our Prophet Muhammad, his family, his companions and those who follow his guidance until the Day of Judgment.

I am very much honored and excited to be elected as the President of Ghana Islamic Society for Education and Reformation (GISER). I do sincerely thank my colleagues for giving me the honor once again to serve the Society with enthusiasm, diligence and devotion as many presidents did before me.

GISER is committed to improving the welfare of the Muslim community through quality and afordable education as well as proper Islamic guidance as declared by the society’s mission. GISER consists of a volunteer board of executive committee and sub committees that manage its various activities.
